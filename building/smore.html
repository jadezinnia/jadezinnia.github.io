Building for Smore
Written on Feb 17, 2026, built during Jul 2025

Smore was a server that I co-own with a lot of my friends under the Azalea Network. It served as both an SMP and a Creative server, offering both experiences. Though we failed to release it in 2025 due to circumstances out of our hands, I worked on both the creative and survival spawns for the server.

SMP World Spawn

The first image shows the foundation of the build - a small little fenced campsite and the Smore logo in the sky. This was not built by myself, it was built by my friend PoikNerd.


The second image displays a house that I built for the zone, next to a pond I also built. The zone's structure was to be a village surrounding this campsite, a basic but unintrusive spawn for our SMP world. I constructed all of the area surrounding the campsite. I even baked some secrets in the houses!

The succeeding images show the complete version of the build in full. As you can see, the zone is small but detailed as to give a good balance between looking nice when a player first joins and also not intruding on their ability to explore the world and get to the area they can break blocks in a quicker fashion.

Creative World Spawn

For the spawn for the creative world, I went with a different aesthetic. Since we were in a plots world, I decided I'd commit to the boundaries of the plot and also try something different. I went with different blocks to imitate a Lego-like palette; opting to go for concrete, concrete powder and wool over grass, moss, dirt, etc.


Just like the survival spawn, this spawn was designed to look nice, be unintrusive and allow the player to exit quickly.

Though Smore is in a limbo state currently, I do hope that it will see a release in the future so that these builds can be used. These builds didn't take that long to make, and are generally minimal as intended.
