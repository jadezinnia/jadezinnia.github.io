The Azalea Network is a Minecraft server network created by my friends and I. (Same people as TDS)

We're hosted on Minehut and aim to provide some cool and unique experiences, such as Airbrush, a painting server — Zombies, a zombie survival server inspired by Call of Duty Zombies and Poke, our refined take on a quality box server.
